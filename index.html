About Us

We Care For Your Health

The SAHARA DRUG DE ADDICTION AND REHABILITATION CENTRE, part of the Spider Educational and Social Welfare Society, is a beacon of hope for those struggling with substance abuse. Since our establishment in 2014, we have been dedicated to providing comprehensive and compassionate care to our patients.

The treatments methods of the centre are classified under clinical and non-clinical methods. Clinical dilemma emerges when we consider the Allopathic treatment of Drug abuse. Non-clinical methods include preventive measures like motivation, family therapy, yoga etc.
